Personal data
What is Flowa?
Flowa is a mapping platform where you can find places that can potentially facilitate virus transmission. With an easy-to-navigate interface, you can see areas of high risk and places with a high rate of virus infections.
How does Flowa work?
It uses bike movement data, scooter locations, and other pedestrian-related data. The data is processed on-the-fly using our custom API, allowing for dynamically selecting the period you want to examine.
Where does Flowa get the data from?
Concerning the flow of people, the data is based on the movement of Oslo City bikes. We use these data to find correlations between increased movement of people and increase in infections.

Concerning the COVID pandemic, the data comes from the publicly available data from the FHI (Folkehelseinstituttet), The Norwegian Institute of Public Health. https://www.fhi.no/en/

We are still looking for new sources of data. You can contribute with your own data to help us build a safer city. To do that go to the subpage Contribute.
Using the map
How can I compare two periods of time?
Use the blue slider under the map to select which month you wish to analyze. The map will update along with the Statistics section. Currently, you can compare each month since April 2020.

Is Flowa available for another city or country?
For now, Flowa is available only for the city of Oslo. The long term objective is to create digital twins for as many cities as possible. We will add new cities in future updates.
