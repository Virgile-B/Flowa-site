We believe that the cities of the future should be developed to accommodate the danger of future pandemics. This is why our purpose is to create a full picture of a pandemic, so any data related to human flow is useful.

The current pandemic taught us a lot about virus transmission prevention. Using this knowledge to first understand and then, possibly, simulate the possible transmission patterns and spread of any virus in the future, can help in managing pandemics in the future and preventing some negative behaviors which occurred in the case of COVID-19.
