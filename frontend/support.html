What is Flowa?
Flowa is a mapping platform where you can find places that can potentially facilitate virus transmission. With an easy-to-navigate interface, you can see areas of high risk and places with a high rate of virus infections.
How does Flowa work?
Using bike movement data, scooter location, and other pedestrian-related data . The data is processed on-the-fly using our custom API, allowing for dinamycally selecting the period you want to examine.
