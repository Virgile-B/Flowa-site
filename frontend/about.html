Get to know us!
Discover who is behind this platform.
Flowa is a mapping platform where you can find places that can potentially facilitate virus transmission. With an easy-to-navigate interface, you can see areas of high risk and places with a high rate of virus infections.
Learn more
What?
Visualization Platform for Understanding Virus Spread
Why?
We want to help people like us
Make citizens conscious about virus spread and help them to avoid places likely to facilitate virus transmission. By collecting and translating the data into comprehensible visual information for everybody. Show the map
Using bike movement data, scooter location, and other pedestrian-related data. The data is processed on-the-fly using our custom API, allowing for dynamically selecting the period you want to examine.
